Frequently Asked Questions

What industries can benefit from blockchain technology?
Blockchain technology can benefit a wide range of industries, including finance, supply chain management, healthcare, real estate, and more. Any industry that requires secure, transparent, and immutable record-keeping can potentially benefit from blockchain integration.
How long does it typically take to develop a custom blockchain solution?
The development time for a custom blockchain solution can vary greatly depending on the complexity of the project. Simple projects might take a few months, while more complex enterprise-level solutions could take 6-12 months or more. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.
What's the difference between a public and private blockchain?
A public blockchain, like Bitcoin or Ethereum, is open for anyone to participate in and view transactions. A private blockchain, on the other hand, is permissioned and only allows selected participants to join the network. Private blockchains are often used by enterprises for better control and privacy.
