Investment Thesis

Our investment thesis is rooted in the belief that AI and blockchain technologies will fundamentally reshape industries and create new market opportunities. We seek to invest in companies that:

  • Leverage AI to create scalable, high-margin SaaS solutions
  • Develop novel blockchain applications that decentralize traditional systems
  • Combine AI and blockchain to create innovative, hybrid solutions
  • Demonstrate a clear path to commercialization and market adoption
