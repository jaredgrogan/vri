The Cybercene

Having studied and worked across leading research institutions in Europe, China, the Middle East, and Africa, Jared’s approach is shaped by the frontiers of global scientific research and innovation.

Motivated by a special opportunity to be a pioneer in higher education in the Arabian Gulf, he contributed centrally to founding two new universities from start-up to maturity, receiving distinction for his leadership with the award of Bunyan Knight in 2018. As Executive Advisor to the Vice Chancellor at NYU Abu Dhabi from 2011 - 2018, he led strategic initiatives and partnerships with stakeholders in academia, government, the private sector, and youth to enhance organizational alignment with UAE goals for nation-building, economic diversification, and societal development.

The recipient of a classical education, Jared was trained in Ancient Greek, Persian, Arabic — and Python. His work “A Cognitive-Neuro Theory of AI Systems” articulates emerging standards of Artificial Intelligence Systems arising out of the ontologies of Artificial Neural Networks rapidly since the 2010s — following stagnant generations of “AI Winter.” Specializing in Computer Vision and Cybernetic Systems Theory, he is a theorist of the “Cybercene.”
