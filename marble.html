Our Thesis

We employ a reverse-induction method, organizing around problem statements led by industry experts. By meeting proven demands validated by industry before we start, we systematically de-risk innovation. Our core focus lies where Decentralization meets AI, primarily targeting B2B and B2G markets, with B2C being ancillary. Across the board, we aim to be an "API Factory" enabling seamless integration and scalability.
