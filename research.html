Commercial Research Groups

The Venture Research Institute (VRI) has established seven specialized Commercial Research Groups, each operating as a "Lab" focused on a key vertical. These labs address industry-specific challenges through advanced AI and decentralized compute technologies, generating MVPs and leveraging expertise for strategic Go-To-Market placement.
