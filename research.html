VRI Research Labs

Commercial Research Groups

The Venture Research Institute (VRI) has established seven specialized Commercial Research Groups, each operating as a "Lab" focused on a key vertical. These labs are dedicated to addressing industry-specific challenges through advanced AI and decentralized compute technologies.

Our labs collaborate closely with industry partners to ensure their research is grounded in real-world needs and drives commercial success. Each lab generates output in the form of Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) and leverages expertise for strategic Go-To-Market (GTM) placement.

Our unique approach ensures that cutting-edge research translates into real-world solutions:

  • Continuous Innovation: Iterative development based on real-world feedback and emerging technologies.
