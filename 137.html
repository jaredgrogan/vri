Company Overview

OneThreeSeven (137) was founded in 2024 by Jared James Grogan to commercialize the groundbreaking, patent-pending A-A-B gate technology. Our mission is to revolutionize computation across multiple industries, making AI more efficient, robotics more responsive, electric vehicles more intelligent, virtual reality more immersive, and paving the way for next-generation optical and photonic computing.

Key Highlights:

  • Multiple revenue streams including licensing, product sales, and consulting
  • Projected to reach unicorn status within 3-4 years

The Technology

The patent-pending A-A-B gate is a novel computational logic gate that utilizes harmonic symmetry in event sequencing, based on the golden ratio. This approach enables:

  • Enhanced processing efficiency
  • Reduced energy consumption
  • Improved scalability in distributed systems
  • Compatibility with emerging optical and photonic computing paradigms

Key Applications:

  3. Smart Grid Optimization

  10. Metaverse NPCs
