How It Works

  1. Transactions are submitted to Chariot's network
  2. Transactions are batched and compressed
  3. Batch proofs are submitted to Ethereum mainnet
  4. Fraud proofs ensure correctness
  5. Users can withdraw assets back to mainnet at any time
